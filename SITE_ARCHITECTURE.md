# Hovoy GRE Pipe Site Architecture

## Positioning

Hovoy should present itself as an engineering-aware industrial supplier, not a catalogue site.
The site needs to guide buyers through three common starting points:

1. Product family is already known
2. Application is known but product path is not final
3. RFQ or supplier review is already in progress

## Homepage wireframe

1. Hero
   Clear positioning, two primary actions, proof points, and one industrial visual.
2. Entry routes
   Start by product family, application, documents, or RFQ.
3. Product families
   Five primary product cards with key use cases.
4. Application sectors
   Four sector cards tied to real service environments.
5. Commercial proof
   Experience, manufacturing, export coordination, and package-completeness signals.
6. Project workflow
   Inquiry review, technical alignment, quotation support, delivery coordination.
7. Contact CTA
   Sticky, visible, and simple.

## Traditional product-line landing architecture

The site should move away from block-style marketing pages and back to the traditional industrial product-line structure used by established manufacturers.

### Product page wireframe

1. Breadcrumb and hero
   Product-line title, short positioning statement, quick actions, and one visual placeholder.
2. Product range
   Diameter range, pressure class, temperature, resin system, installation environment, and conductivity route.
3. Product series
   Acid anhydride, amine, conductive, non-conductive, or other real product families under the main line.
4. Product overview
   Basic technical and commercial logic behind the product line.
5. Applications
   Typical use environments and service media.
6. Jointing systems
   DSJ thread, key-lock, bonding, flange, transition, and a placeholder for connection diagrams.
7. Fittings and accessories
   Elbows, tees, reducers, flanges, couplings, nipples, and other linked components.
8. Supply scope
   Straight pipe, fittings, fabricated items, export packing, and package discussion.
9. Documentation and downloads
   Datasheet, range table, jointing sheet, and RFQ support placeholders.
10. Manufacturing and quality
   Production capability, testing capability, tooling, shipment readiness, and image placeholders.
11. RFQ
   Traditional quotation inputs and a direct path to contact.

## SEO principles for all pages

- Write only people-first, buyer-useful copy
- Tie keywords to applications, media, and project tasks
- Use internal links between products, applications, resources, and contact
- Add breadcrumb and product structured data where relevant
- Prefer pre-rendered or server-rendered HTML for indexable pages
- Add real proof assets: certifications, projects, factory, packing, inspection
